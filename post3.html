The Best Time to Visit Popular Cities

By WanderWorld Travel | Updated: November 2025

Timing your travels right can make all the difference — from better weather to fewer crowds and cheaper prices. Here’s when to visit some of the world’s most popular cities.

1. Paris, France

Visit in April–June or September–October for mild weather, fewer tourists, and beautiful gardens in full bloom.

2. New York City, USA

Spring and autumn offer the perfect balance of warm days and crisp evenings — plus amazing events like the NYC Marathon.

3. Tokyo, Japan

March and April are cherry blossom season — a stunning time to experience Japan’s culture and beauty.

4. Cape Town, South Africa

November to March offers the best beach weather, but April–May is perfect for wine tours and fewer crowds.

5. Rome, Italy

Go in late spring or early autumn for comfortable weather and shorter lines at famous landmarks.
